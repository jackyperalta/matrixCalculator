Unary Operations
    Determinant of A: det(A)
    Determinant of B: det(B)
    A Transpose: A^T
    B Transpose: B^T
    Inverse of A: A^-1
    Inverse of B: B^-1
    Integer power of A when A is a square matrix: A^n for 1 ≤ n ≤ 10
    Integer power of B when B is a square matrix: B^n for 1 ≤ n ≤ 10

Binary Operations
    Multiply A and B: AB
    Multiply B and A: BA
